KaiSTEM · Track One · Science

🔬 Science — guess, then find out

Hands-on inquiry: water, plants, colour, bugs, shadows. The loop advances the kind of prediction the child makes — from a position, to a change over time, to a transformation. Every project teaches the parent how to run it with Kai.

Ages ~3–5· ~20–30 min/day· Parent-led · screen-light

The whole idea in one line

You don't deliver facts — you narrate curiosity. The Science loop runs the same five moves every project, so it feels effortless for both of you: guess first, then find out, and joy over correctness.

The Science loop — five moves, every time

  • 👀Notice
  • 💭Wonder
  • 🤔Predict
  • 🧪Test
  • 💬Talk

Same five moves in every project. What advances is the kind of prediction — a position (up/down), then a change over time, then a transformation.

The five projects

01 · ★☆☆

Sink or Float?

Five days at the water tub. Learn the core move — guess, then test — plus sorting and counting. Predicts a position (up/down).

02 · ★★☆

Will It Grow?

Plant beans in clear cups and watch over two weeks. Introduces the fair test (change one thing) and observing change over time.

03 · ★★★

Kitchen Color Lab

Color mixing and the big baking-soda fizz. The leap to predicting a transformation — what something will turn into.

04 · ★★★

Backyard Bug Safari

Hunt, observe up close, and group living things. Adds classifying — sorting bugs by legs, wings, and where they live.

05 · ★★★★

Shadow Play

Catch, change, and measure shadows — then discover they move with the sun. Adds measuring & comparing and spotting a pattern over time.
