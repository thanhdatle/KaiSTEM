# 0001 — Mission set & starting baseline

- **Date:** 2026-06-23
- **Status:** active
- **Type:** mission / baseline

## Context

First session. Workspace created from scratch. Parent wants to teach Kai (3.5) STEM.

## What we established

- **Mission:** spark lifelong curiosity + quality bonding time. Joy > correctness.
  (See [MISSION.md](../HUB.md).)
- **Learner model:** the *parent* is the reader/learner here; Kai is the scientist. Lessons
  are parent-facing activity guides.
- **Kai's interests (3.5):** animals & nature, water/mess/mixing, numbers/books/screens.
  Lukewarm on building/vehicles.
- **Format constraints (parent's words):** project-based, ~30 min hands-on daily, note-keeping
  required, progressive difficulty, parent preps physical materials in advance.

## Decisions (non-obvious, may be revised)

1. **Core method = Notice → Wonder → Predict → Test → Talk**, a toddler-sized version of the
   5E inquiry model. Chosen because it's the through-line that makes every future project
   feel familiar to Kai and easy for the parent. Captured in the Parent Playbook reference.
2. **Every project ends with a "Kai leads" day** — to build ownership / STEM identity, not
   just compliance.
3. **Counting woven in** when natural (early math is the strongest predictor of later success
   per NAEYC).
4. **Journal = the retention + bonding artifact.** Predict-then-result side by side; parent
   scribes Kai's exact words.

## Baseline (assumed, UNVERIFIED — confirm after Project 1)

- Attention span: ~10–20 min on a high-interest task. (Lessons say 30 min but that includes
  setup/mess; real focused time is the thing to measure.)
- Can: predict simple cause/effect, sort by one attribute, count small sets.
- Cannot yet: absorb abstract explanation (e.g. *why* things float — buoyancy). We do NOT teach
  the mechanism; we build the predict→test habit.

## Open / to confirm next session

- How long did Kai actually stay engaged? (Calibrates future session length.)
- Did the predict-first habit start transferring outside the activity?
- Which interest pulled hardest — steer Project 2 toward it.
- Pick a parent community for "wisdom" layer (candidates in RESOURCES.md).
